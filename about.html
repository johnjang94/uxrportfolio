New Brunswick Fredericton

In 2017, I moved to here in Toronto --- it was meant to be a visit, but I ended up working as a receptionist.
It could have been a good position for me, but I didn't really enjoy the position back then.

After leaving the job, I pursued my interested into the field of Education and have spent the next few years studying and working as a teacher --- until COVID-19 came out of nowhere.

Things have become a lot tougher and I needed computer to continue teaching the students.
Eventually, this type of working environment has opened my eyes on user experience (really because I was always thinking about what I am using given tools to teach and how I should teach young students to make sure that they are staying engaged).

This was the moment I thought that I want to pursue my career in the field of UX.

In January 2022, I have enrolled myself to a UX Design bootcamp that is offered by one of the accredited university here in the local area. The program lasted the total of 8 months and I have spent last 2 months practicing the UX skills I learned while participating in a real-world practice case with other teammates from the program.

Today, I hold an official UX Certificate while continuing the practice on my own.
