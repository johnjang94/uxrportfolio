Research

After I finished exploring TuGo insurance company's product, I have conducted an initial interview with the participants I have recruited. The interview was conducted via Zoom. When the interview was completed, I have analyzed the results using Apple's Excel product, Numbers.

Insights

Contact page

  • contact page should be readily appear on the website
  • contact page needs to be simplified with the most important and useful information

    • ×
    Q. What did we learn?

    According to the participants, the following information is great to see:

    the contact page has a separate page link for 'Frequently Asked Questions'

    there is a form that the customers can write an inquiry instantly

    On the other hand, they were not feeling comfortable with the following obstacles:

    they did not expect to find the contact information from “About Us” menu

    the contact number for general inquiry is the same number for emergency purposes - why is the number addressed twice?

    Navigation Menu Layout

  • the buttons should be re-organized
  • Contact page needs to be simplified with the most important and useful information.

    • ×

    Q. What did we learn?

  • most of the participants did not understand why there were a combination of two layers of menu layouts and the hamburger menu staying together on the page.
  • according to them, it is much appearing better if the company sticked to the menu layouts or to the hamburger menu.

    • Claim page

  • this page was more like displaying an instruction to the participants (DIY) rather than showing them where the papers could be submitted.

    • ×

    Q. What did I do?

    I have created a new flow of pages where the current and potential customers can upload the required paperwork without making a separate contact with the company
