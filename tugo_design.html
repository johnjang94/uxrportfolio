Design

Considerations - 3 points

I have considered the following points while coming up with the new design:

  • simplicity
    • it is important to keep things simple and intuitive for the customers to follow.
  • ease on navigation
    • it is important to remember that the customers prefer easy challenges.

    Branding & Style

    The brand tone for TuGo travel insurance is soft and entertaining to enlighten the customers with excitement.
    Therefore, I have been consistent with blue and green, which are the primary colours for the design, as well as the font style and the font size.

    Visual Drafts

    Pages for the web app were sketched based on the user flow to get a better sense of how they would look and after the sketches were finalized.

    Sketched Changes in the Menu bar

  • taking an input from the participants, I have replace some of the buttons.
  • taking an input from the participants, I have created the drop-down menu that's hidden.

    • Sketched Changes in Contact Us page

  • instead of keeping all the information, the new design displays three most important ways to contact the company.
  • the new design displays the information about the hours of operation below the three-option icons, so that the customers understand when they can reach out or not.
  • the new design does not really contain the information about the office location as it did not seem important to most of the customers.

    • Sketched Changes in Claims page

  • In the original design, “Claims” page only was available in one of the web pages as an instruction. The original product did not have any portal to upload the documents.
  • The new design contains the portal where the current customers can upload the related documents to claim for a coverage.
