    Context & Background

    TuGo insurance company is a Canadian-owned corporation that offers financial assistance on medical coverage during trips or on car rentals.

    They mainly help Canadians, but they also offer assistance to international students or travelers under conditions depending on the circumstances.

    There were two major complaints among the customers:

  • it is difficult to find the contact page when they need to reach out to the company in an urgent situation
  • it is not convenient that the customers need to send an email to the customer service in order to claim for a coverage


    • Insurance page Claims page

    Contact Us page About Us page

    Recently, many customers who have tried TuGo service raised issues regarding the process of claiming for the coverage, addressing how difficult and complicated it feels to go through. Hence, I have decided to modify the process.
